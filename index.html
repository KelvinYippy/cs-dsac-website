What is DSAC?

DSAC is the Computer Science Departmental Student Advisory Committee. The committee's primary task is to represent Yale CS students to the faculty and administration, meeting with the Director of Undegraduate Studies and Department Chair several times a year. The committee consists of at least five undergraduate CS majors who are appointed or elected by popular vote each year. For the 2021-2022 academic year, the committee is:
